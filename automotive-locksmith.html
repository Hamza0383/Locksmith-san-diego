Situations Our Auto Locksmiths Handle

Keys Locked Inside the Car

This is one of the most common calls we get. You set your keys on the seat, close the door, and hear that click. It happens to everyone. Our automotive locksmith in San Diego uses safe, non-destructive methods to open your door. No broken windows. No scratched frames. No damage at all in most cases. We get your door open fast and you're on your way.

Lost or Stolen Car Keys With No Spare

Losing your only set of car keys is a stressful situation. If they were stolen, it's even more urgent. We can make a replacement key for most vehicles — even without the original. We use your vehicle identification number to cut and program a new key that works just like the factory one. It's faster and cheaper than going to the dealership.

Broken or Worn Ignition That Won't Turn

Sometimes the problem isn't the key — it's the ignition. A worn ignition cylinder can make it hard or impossible to start your car. We repair and replace ignition cylinders for a wide range of vehicles right there in the parking lot or on the street. You don't need to tow your car anywhere. We handle it on the spot.

Types of Automotive Locksmith Services

Transponder Key and Smart Key Programming

Most modern cars use transponder keys. These keys have a chip inside that talks to your car's computer. If the chip isn't programmed correctly, the car won't start — even if the key fits the lock. We program transponder keys for most makes and models. We use professional-grade equipment to get the programming right every time.

Key Fob Replacement and Synchronization

Lost your key fob or remote? We replace and sync key fobs for most vehicles. A new fob needs to be paired to your car before it works. We handle that programming on the spot. No need to order one from the dealer and wait a week for an appointment to get it set up.

Ignition Cylinder Repair and Replacement

A failing ignition is more than an inconvenience. It can leave you stranded without warning. We replace ignition cylinders for cars, trucks, and SUVs. We match the new cylinder to your existing keys when possible. That way you don't need a whole new set of keys just because your ignition gave out.
