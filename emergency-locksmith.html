Common Emergency Situations We Respond To

Locked Out of Your Home Late at Night

This is one of the most common calls we get. You come home after a long day, reach for your keys, and they're gone. Or you stepped outside for a minute and the door locked behind you. It's frustrating. It's stressful. And it can feel scary, especially late at night.

We get to you fast. We open your door without breaking anything. And we make sure your lock still works properly when we leave. If you want to add a spare key option or upgrade your lock while we're there, we'll walk you through your choices.

Keys Snapped Inside the Lock

A broken key stuck in a lock is a tricky problem. You can't open the door, and you can't pull the key out without the right tools. Trying to dig it out yourself usually makes things worse. Our technicians carry key extraction tools and know exactly how to remove a broken key without damaging the lock cylinder.

Once the key is out, we can cut you a new one on the spot if needed. Most of the time, the whole thing is sorted in under an hour.

Forced Entry Damage Needing Immediate Lock Repair

If someone broke into your home or business, your lock may be damaged or completely destroyed. That's a security risk you can't leave overnight. We respond to post-break-in calls quickly. We assess the damage, replace what needs replacing, and get your property secured again before we leave.

We keep a range of lock hardware in our vans so we can handle most replacements the same visit.
