Complete Home Lock Services in San Diego

Lock Installation, Replacement, and Upgrades

Your home's locks are the first line of defense. If they're old, worn, or just not up to the job, it's time to do something about it. We install new locks, replace damaged ones, and upgrade hardware that's past its prime. We stock a wide range of options — from solid deadbolts to heavy-duty entry sets — so you get the right fit for your door and your budget.

Rekeying Your Home Locks Quickly and Affordably

Rekeying is one of the most practical things a homeowner can do. When you rekey a lock, we change the internal pins so that old keys no longer work. Your existing lock stays in place, but only your new keys open it. It costs less than a full lock replacement and takes less time too. If you've moved into a new home, had a contractor in recently, or lost a key, rekeying is the smart move.

Smart Lock and Keypad Entry Installation

More San Diego homeowners are switching to smart locks and keypad entry systems. No more digging for keys. No more wondering if you locked the door before you left. We install and configure these systems so they work properly from day one. We also make sure you understand how to use them before we leave.

When Do You Need a Residential Locksmith?

Moving Into a New Home or Apartment in San Diego

This is one of the best times to call a residential locksmith in San Diego. You have no idea who has a copy of your current keys. The previous owner, their real estate agent, a neighbor, or an old contractor — any one of them could still have access. Rekeying or replacing your locks right after you move in is a simple step that gives you real peace of mind.

After a Break-In or Attempted Forced Entry

A break-in is a violation. Beyond the shock of it, you're left with a damaged lock and a home that doesn't feel secure. We respond to post-break-in calls quickly. We assess what's broken, replace what needs replacing, and make sure everything is solid before we go. We also look at the surrounding hardware to make sure nothing else was compromised.

Lost Keys or Locks That No Longer Work Properly

Lost keys are more than just an inconvenience. If they fell into the wrong hands, your home may not be as safe as you think. And locks that stick, jam, or feel loose are a sign that something needs attention. Don't wait for a full failure. Call us early and we'll sort it out before it becomes a bigger problem.

Types of Home Lock Services We Offer

Deadbolt Installation and High-Security Lock Options

A good deadbolt is one of the most effective security upgrades you can make. We install single and double-cylinder deadbolts, grade-1 locks, and high-security options from trusted brands. If you live in an area of San Diego where break-ins are a concern — or just want the added confidence of stronger hardware — we'll help you pick the right option.

Sliding Door and Window Lock Reinforcement

Sliding glass doors are a weak point in many San Diego homes. The standard locks that come on them are often easy to defeat. We reinforce sliding door frames, add secondary locking bars, and install security pins that stop doors from being lifted off the track. Window locks can be upgraded too. These are small changes that make a real difference.

Master Key Systems for Multi-Unit Properties

If you own a duplex, small apartment building, or multi-unit property in San Diego, a master key system makes life much easier. One key opens every unit. Each tenant still has their own key that only works on their door. We design and install these systems for property owners across the county — from small two-unit buildings to larger residential complexes.
