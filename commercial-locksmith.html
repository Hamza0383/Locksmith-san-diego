Common Commercial Lock Situations We Handle

Rekeying After Employee Turnover or Termination

When a staff member leaves, your locks need to change. You don't know how many key copies they made. Rekeying is the fastest fix. We change the internal pins so old keys stop working. Your team gets fresh keys. The whole process is quick and costs far less than replacing the locks. We can do one door or an entire building in one visit.

Upgrading Locks on a Newly Leased Commercial Space

Moving into a new office or retail space? The old tenants had keys too. So did their staff and contractors. Before you bring in your equipment or inventory, get the locks sorted. It's a simple step. It protects everything you're about to move in.

Emergency Lockout at Your Business Location

Getting locked out of your own business wastes time and money. Every minute outside is a minute lost. We respond fast to business lockouts all across San Diego. We get you back inside without damaging your door. We also check that everything works properly before we leave.

Our Commercial Locksmith Services

Master Key and Keyway Systems for Buildings

A master key system keeps things simple. Managers carry one key that opens every door. Staff get keys that only work on the doors they need. We design and install these systems for offices, retail centers, and apartment buildings across San Diego County. It keeps access clean and easy to manage.

Electronic Access Control and Keypad Entry

Key cards, PIN pads, and fob systems are now common in San Diego businesses. They're more secure than standard keys. They also make it easy to add or remove access without changing locks. We install and program these systems for single doors and full buildings. We also fix them when they stop working.

Panic Bars, Exit Devices, and ADA-Compliant Hardware

Commercial buildings in San Diego must meet safety and access codes. That includes panic bars on exit doors, lever handles, and proper door closers. We install and service all of this. If you're not sure what your building requires, we can walk your property and explain what applies to you.
