What We Do

We Handle Lock and Key Needs for Three Types of Customers

Home. Car. Business. One team, one call, everything handled.

Homeowners

Home Lock and Key Service

Locked out? Moving into a new place? Want better locks on your doors? We rekey, replace, and upgrade home locks across San Diego every day. Most jobs are done in a single visit.

Businesses

Commercial Lock and Access Work

Rekeying after staff changes, master key systems, access control installs, and commercial lockouts. We work quickly and with little disruption to your operations.

Drivers

Automotive Key and Lockout Service

Keys lost, locked inside the car, or broken? We make replacement keys on the spot for most vehicle makes and models. No tow. No dealership wait.
